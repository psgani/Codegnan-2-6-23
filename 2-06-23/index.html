Tahira Kashyap- An Inspiration for Millions

Tahira Kashyap

Tahira Kashyap is an Indian Writer, Professor, and Theatre Director. She is the wife of well known Bollywood Actor, Ayushmann Khurrana. Let’s dig out some more information on Tahira’s lifeTahira Kashyap was born on 21 January 1985 (age 35; as in 2018) in Chandigarh, India. By profession, Tahira is a Writer, Professor, and Theatre Director. Tahira’s writing debut started with her novel “I Promise”, which is a story about a perfectionist.Tahira studied from Yadavindra Public School, Mohali, and is an alumna of Punjab University, Chandigarh, India. She has a Masters Degree in Mass Communication. In September 2018, Tahira took to Instagram and informed that she was detected with Ductal Carcinoma In Situ in her right breast with high grade malignant cells and had undergone mastectomy. Later her husband, Ayushmann Khurrana confirmed that she is fine.

Family

Tahira Kashyap was born on January 21, 1983 (36 Years old as of 2019) Chandigarh, India. She hails from a Hindu hmin family and have completed her school from Yadavindra public school, Mohali, India. She did graduation from Punjab University, Chandigarh and post graduated in mass communication. Tahira’s father’s name is Yajan Kashyap and her mother’s name is Anita Kashyap. Tahira is married to the famous actor named Ayushmann Khurrana after being in a courtship for 12 years. They both are blessed with two children, a daughter and a son named Varushka (April 21. 2014), Virajveer (January 2, 2012). The couple tied the knot in 2011.

Achievements

Tahira Kashyap is a multi-talented woman. Since, Chandigarh college, she used to be an active member of the theatre group and also the theatre groups outside of her college as well. At the initial stage, Tahira started working at Mithibai College, Mumbai, India as an assistant professor. Later on, she also began to teach at R.D. national college and W.A. science college. Kashyap was a professor in L S Raheja college in Mumbai as well. Tahira was the programming head at BIG 92.7 FM. In 2011, she made her debut by writing a novel titled “ A Promise… A Story of Love And Deceit. Which was released on March 2 by the Apps Publishers. In 2012, her second book published called “Souled Out” which was published by Pustak Mahal. On 15 February 2015, “Cracking the Code: A Journey to Bollywood” co-authored with her husband Ayushmann Khurrana was published by Rupa Publications India.
